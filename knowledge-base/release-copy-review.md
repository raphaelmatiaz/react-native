# Release Copy Review (Task 16)

## Tone Standards
- Calm, supportive, and non-shaming language.
- Relapse framed as data and learning, not failure.
- No fear-based or manipulative wording.
- Premium upsell is optional and never in crisis contexts.

## Reviewed Copy Areas
- Home: identity reinforcement, relapse flow wording, and premium prompt.
- Craving flow: normalization, ritual guidance, completion reinforcement.
- Progress: metrics and healing timeline language.
- Profile/Premium/Settings: practical, non-judgmental guidance.
- Notifications: short, grounded reminders without pressure.

## Copy Decisions
- Keep "One cigarette is data, not failure" in relapse contexts.
- Keep "You are becoming smoke-free" identity framing in daily reminders.
- Keep premium descriptions capability-focused, not guilt-based.

## Follow-up Before Store Submission
- One final read on-device for truncation and line-break quality.
- Verify no urgency language appears in premium or notification copy.
