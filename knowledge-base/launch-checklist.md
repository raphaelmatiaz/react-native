# Launch Checklist and Post-Launch Metrics (Task 16)

## Pre-Launch Checklist
- [ ] Confirm iOS `bundleIdentifier` and Android `package` values.
- [ ] Finalize app icon and splash assets for store submissions.
- [ ] Verify `eas.json` profiles for `development`, `preview`, `production`.
- [ ] Run lint, typecheck, tests, and production export.
- [ ] Perform smoke pass on at least 1 iOS and 1 Android device.
- [ ] Verify onboarding, relapse/recovery, notifications, premium restore/purchase paths.
- [ ] Confirm local-only privacy copy in app and store description.

## Post-Launch Metric Tracking Plan
- Day 1, 3, 7 retention.
- Average cravings logged per active user.
- Craving resist rate (`resisted=true` / total cravings).
- Relapse events per active user and recovery completion rate.
- Notification opt-in rate and daily notification delivery health.
- Premium conversion at 7-day, 30-day, and 20 resisted-craving thresholds.
- Crash-free sessions and startup crash rate.

## Monitoring Cadence
- First 72h: monitor twice daily.
- Week 1: monitor daily.
- Week 2+: monitor weekly with trend notes.
